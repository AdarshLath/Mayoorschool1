Campus Life

Creative Center for Tiny Tot

At Mayoor we believe in imparting expertise to our children in all fields right from the grass root level. Therefore, the school has a spacious and well-equipped activity room to train the young children hone their talent and unleash their creativity.

Horse riding

Horse Riding as a sport facility is provided in a ground area specifically dedicated to horse riding under the supervision of qualified trainers. Along with the training to ride a horse, children are also trained for various equestrian events. Some of the students have won championships and trophies at the state and national level. The display of the equestrian feats by these state and national level players is always a treat to watch on the School Sports Day.

Smart Class Rooms

In this age, computers play a big role in our education.We all know that if a topic is explained through a visual method, it becomes much easier for the students to understand.Each smart room in the school is equipped with LCD projector, Interactive Digital Board System, center CPU and UPS. All classrooms are connected via networking to the known center, the server with the large repository of digital content mapped to the curriculum.

Multi Purpose Hall

The school has a modern, well-equipped Multi Purpose Hall with a seating capacity of 500 students and latest projection system and audio facility. This hall is an ideal place to conduct multifarious activities like debate, discussions, quizzes, workshops etc.

Achievements

Dr.APJ Young Scientist Competition
Scored 100/100 in Dr.APJ Young Scientist Competition

Brilliant Mayoorian, Jaiditya Dev of class 12 A brought huge laurels to Mayoor School, Noida by scoring 100/100 marks in Dr.APJ young scientist competition. He has secured the second position and has been awarded with a cellphone.

Online Poetry Recitation Challenge
won 2nd prize in Online Poetry Recitation Challenge

Talented Mayoorian, Dhwani Anand of VI C brought laurels to Mayoor School Noida by securing the second position in Online Poetry Recitation Challenge in the Under 12 age category. She was also awarded a certificate of appreciation and a cash prize. The event held, on 26 July 2020, was organised by MELIO and witnessed about 190 students from India and abroad participating in four different age categories.

Final Round of KVPY -2019
successfully clearing the final round of KISHORE VAIGYANIK PROTSAHAN YOJANA-2019

Deeptansh Sharma of XI C has brought huge laurels to Mayoor School, Noida by successfully clearing the final round of KISHORE VAIGYANIK PROTSAHAN YOJANA-2019. He will be awarded scholarships worth Rs. 80,000 per year for B.Sc. and Rs. 1,04, 000 per year for M.Sc. and PhD as per UGC norms. After this commendable achievement, he is also entitled to get direct admissions to IISC and IISER.
